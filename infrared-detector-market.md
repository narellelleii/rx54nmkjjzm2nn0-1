<p><ul><li>Uncooled Infrared Detector</li><li>Cooled Infrared Detector</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The infrared detector market is primarily divided into two types: uncooled and cooled infrared detectors. Uncooled infrared detectors operate at ambient temperatures, making them smaller and more cost-effective, often utilized in applications such as surveillance and fire detection. Cooled infrared detectors, on the other hand, require cooling to enhance sensitivity, providing superior performance in detecting low-level infrared signals, commonly used in military, aerospace, and high-end industrial applications. Each type serves distinct needs based on performance and application requirements.</p></p>
