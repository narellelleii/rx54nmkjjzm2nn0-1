<p><p>The wastewater decanters market encompasses various types, including centrifugal decanters, floating decanters, and others. Centrifugal decanters utilize centrifugal force to separate solids from liquids efficiently, often employed in industrial operations. Floating decanters, on the other hand, operate by allowing lighter materials to rise, effectively separating them from denser liquids. Other types may include various designs tailored for specific applications, such as gravity decanters and specialized systems for different wastewater treatment processes, enhancing overall efficiency and resource recovery.</p></p>
